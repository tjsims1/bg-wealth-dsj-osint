FORENSIC NOTES (added by investigator, NOT from broadcast):

1. STRONGEST SECURITIES-FRAUD PREDICATE TO DATE — VERBATIM:
   "As long as you follow Professor Stephen's trading method, the BG
   team will 100% guarantee your profit."
   This is an unconditional written guarantee of investment profit
   distributed to retail victims by operators of an unregistered
   investment offering. The 100% framing eliminates any "good-faith
   belief" defense — guarantee language at this level is per se
   indicative of fraud under longstanding SEC and FTC enforcement
   doctrine. Combined with the §3.4.2 Vietnamese-Zoom advertisement of
   "1.8% daily profit, capital doubles in 60 days, BG guarantees 100%",
   this satisfies the predicate elements for 15 U.S.C. § 77q(a)
   without further investigative work.

2. MLM "TWO PRINCIPLES" = COERCION MECHANICS:
   - "Respect": obligation to reply promptly (victim availability for
     operator outreach, especially during withdrawal-blocking events).
   - "Gratitude": obligation to actively recruit ("take the initiative
     to share the investment projects... invite more people to join").
   Recruitment-as-moral-duty framing is a textbook MLM-pyramid
   coercion mechanism. The structural effect is to make declining to
   recruit feel like a betrayal of the group rather than a personal
   choice.

3. KYC-IMPERSONATION + WALLET-FREEZE ATTACK VECTOR:
   "All users will be subject to identity verification when using a
   new wallet address for the first time to withdraw funds... The BG
   team will contact you for confirmation."
   This serves three operator purposes:
   a. Harvest additional PII from victims at the withdrawal-attempt
      moment (when victim is most motivated to comply with anything
      the operators ask).
   b. Manufacture an indefinitely-extensible delay window (the
      "review" is operator-controlled, no defined deadline).
   c. Allow operators to flag and block withdrawals destined for
      forensically-traceable wallets — e.g. known exchange-deposit
      addresses, attorneys' wallets, fraud-recovery escrow services.
   Combined with the wallet-change-approval gate ("contact the head
   of the BG team to submit an application"), victims are prevented
   from re-routing their fake balance to any operator-disapproved
   destination.
