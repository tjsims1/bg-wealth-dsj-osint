The Trading & Compounding Engine

BG Wealth Sharing utilizes a highly sophisticated Binary Options Trading system to generate daily income for its members. The process is designed to be accessible, requiring zero prior trading knowledge from the user.

Daily Signal Operations

Members receive exactly two trading signals daily via the Bonchat application. These signals operate on the BTC/USDT pair and are timed specifically at 6:30 PM and 10:30 PM.

  • Incredible Accuracy: The system boasts a 99.6% accuracy rate.
  • Risk Management: Only 1% of your capital is used per trade.
  • Return Rate: Successful trades yield a 2X return on the traded amount.

For example, if you hold a $300 account, the 1% trade size is $3. A successful signal returns $6. From the $3 profit, 60% ($1.80) is your direct profit, and 40% goes into the company's reward system. This generates a daily passive income between 1.3% and 1.8%.

The 55-Day Compounding Magic

"By retaining your daily profits within the system rather than withdrawing, the magic of compounding takes effect."

According to BG Wealth algorithms, allowing your account to compound will double your total capital (2X) in exactly 55 days. A $300 account becomes $600 without any additional deposits, purely through consistent daily execution.

Withdrawals & Security Rules

To maintain the integrity and longevity of the £30 Million guarantee fund, BG Wealth and DSJ Exchange enforce strict, transparent withdrawal rules. The system utilizes a dual-wallet architecture.

The Dual Wallet System

1. Trade Wallet

Holds your initial deposit and daily trading profit. Used for executing the 2 daily Bonchat signals.

2. Exchange Wallet

Holds your network rewards: Welcome Bonuses, Referral/Sponsor Bonuses, and Cycle Bonuses.

Withdrawal Deductions & Rules

Withdrawals are processed instantly via Cryptocurrency (USDT, BTC, Ethereum). There are no manual delays, but specific deduction rules apply to ensure ecosystem health:

  • Trade Wallet (Pre-2X): If you withdraw from your Trade Wallet *before* your initial capital has doubled (reached 2X), a 20% deduction is applied.
  • Trade Wallet (Post-2X): Once your account naturally compounds to 2X, withdrawals from the Trade Wallet incur a 0% deduction.
  • Exchange Wallet: All withdrawals from the Exchange Wallet (Team & Referral income) carry a flat 12% deduction.

"The system is designed to reward patience. By waiting the 55 days for your account to reach 2X, you avoid the 20% early-withdrawal deduction entirely."
